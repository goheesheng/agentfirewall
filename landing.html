// the problem

Two ways your agent's wallet gets drained.
Both are unfixed in the x402 spec.

FAILURE MODE 01

Wallets have no spending controls.

Per-call cap, daily budget, allow-listed sellers — none of it exists at the keypair layer. One bad prompt drains the wallet.

"my x402 wallet just autopaid for a vpn subscription i forgot i even had. it didn't flinch."— @ClankerOnChain · May 7, 2026
FAILURE MODE 02

Sellers can lie about prices.

The seller alone fills PaymentRequirements.amount. Discovery says $0.01; live 402 charges $1.00. The agent just signs.

"PaymentRequirements.amount is set unilaterally by the seller. A malicious endpoint can publish $0.01 in the Bazaar and charge $1.00 live."— x402 architectural critique · Layer 1 #5
// how it works

An in-process proxy. Two locks. Both must pass.

Drop AgentFirewall in front of your agent's HTTP client. Every x402 call runs through the proxy. The wallet keypair lives inside the firewall — agents can't bypass it. Block decisions land before any signature is produced.

agent→ AgentFirewall→ budget?→ discovery?→ sig verify?→ price match?→ sign & pay·or· HTTP 451
LOCK 01 · SPEND POLICY

Per-call cap · daily budget · allow-list.

Stops Grok-style prompt injection drains. Five named templates (paranoid → open). Daily budget rolls at UTC midnight.

  • Blocks calls over your per-call cap
  • Tracks daily spend with rollover
  • Hosts must be on the allow-list
  • Templates: paranoid · researcher · trader · default · open
LOCK 02 · QUOTE-BINDING (Ed25519)

Verifies the seller's signed price.

Fetches the seller's .well-known/x402.json, verifies the Ed25519 signature, compares discovery price to live 402 price. Multi-facilitator.

  • Closes Layer 1 #5 quote-mismatch attack
  • 60s discovery cache
  • Configurable price tolerance
  • Verifies any seller signature, not just CDP-issued

The questions every paying customer asks.

Why $9 instead of free?

Free tier exists — self-host the MIT repo. The $9/mo founder seat buys hosted infra, the dashboard, alerts, and a real 20-minute onboarding call.

What chain does this support?

Solana today (devnet shipping, mainnet next week). Base support is on the roadmap — the Ed25519 verification logic is chain-agnostic.

Why won't Coinbase ship this in CDP?

They might. But CDP would only verify CDP-issued seller signatures. AgentFirewall is multi-facilitator — verifies any seller's signed quote. As facilitators proliferate, the policy layer must live agent-side.

What's the signed-quote spec?

A draft minimal format: {price, currency, validUntil, sellerPubkey, signature} served at .well-known/x402.json. PR to x402-foundation/x402 lands this week.

Does my wallet leave my machine?

Self-host: no. Hosted: yes — a per-customer keypair is provisioned in our cluster. We sign your encryption envelope with your control key, you can rotate any time. Enterprise gets BYO key.

Refund policy?

7 days, no questions. If the firewall doesn't catch a bad call in week one, the rest is free.
